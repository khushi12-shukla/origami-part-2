1. a square piece of paper in half diagonally.
  2. Fold it in half again.
  3. pull back the fold you just made and you will see it has a crease line.
  4. the top of the paper, bring the tip and and fold it to the middle tip.
  5. same on the bottom of the paper. Bringing the bottom tip to the middle tip.
  6. whole paper over and fold the tip of the paper half way down
  7. paper 90 degrees and fold the top tip down.
  8. fold the bottom of the paper over spreading the two inner pages out.
  9. Stand it up and add any more details if you want. For example, we added a small white circle to put onto the belly and small white triangles to go on top of the ears.
  10. felt tip pen to add the eyes and nose, and there you have it, your very own Wolf!
